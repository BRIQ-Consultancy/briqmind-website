Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the principles regarding the processing, protection, and your rights related to personal data obtained during the use of BRIQ Mind’s digital products and services. BRIQ Mind attaches a high level of importance to user privacy and undertakes to ensure the security of personal data in compliance with applicable legislation (primarily Law No. 6698 KVKK and, to the extent applicable, the GDPR).

1. Scope

This policy covers the processing of personal data collected via the domain briqmind.com and its subdomains, API services linked to this site, integrations, customer services, and technical support channels. The privacy policies of third-party service providers apply separately.

  • Web platform: briqmind.com and subpages
  • API and integrations (e.g., form processing, email service)
  • Customer services and technical support communication channels

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Special Categories of Personal Data: Data such as race, ethnic origin, political opinion, health, biometric, and similar data.
  • Data Controller: The natural/legal person who determines the purposes and means of processing (BRIQ Mind).
  • Data Processor: The third person/entity that processes personal data on behalf of the data controller.
  • Explicit Consent: Consent given freely, informed, and specific to the subject matter.

4. Categories of Data Collected

  • Identity Information: Name, surname, (if any) username.
  • Contact Information: Email, phone, company name (contact form, etc.).
  • Transaction and Session Information: IP address, browser and device information, operating system, date/time stamps, referrer page.
  • Behavioral Data: Page views, click and interaction records, features used, search history.
  • Identifiers (Cookies): Session management, remembering preferences, performance measurement.
  • Content Data: Form contents and attachments you upload/fill in on the platform.

5. Data Collection Methods and Sources

  • Directly from you: Forms, email, support requests, contract processes.
  • By automated means: Cookies, browser/device records, security logs.
  • From third parties: Services we integrate with (only to the extent necessary).

6. Purposes of Processing

  • Responding to user requests, managing support and customer relationships.
  • Providing, maintaining, improving, and personalizing our services.
  • Ensuring system security, preventing fraud and abuse.
  • Conducting analysis and reporting; evaluating performance, usage, and trends.
  • Fulfilling our legal obligations and responding to requests from competent authorities.
  • Sending marketing and announcement communications (only with your consent; you can opt out at any time).
  • Testing and optimizing our AI models with anonymized/pseudonymous data.

7. Legal Bases

Your personal data may be processed on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Explicit consent (KVKK Art.5/1, GDPR Art.6/1-a)
  • Necessity for the establishment/performance of a contract (KVKK Art.5/2-c, GDPR Art.6/1-b)
  • Legal obligation (KVKK Art.5/2-ç, GDPR Art.6/1-c)
  • Legitimate interest (KVKK Art.5/2-f, GDPR Art.6/1-f), provided that your fundamental rights and freedoms are not harmed
  • The establishment, exercise, or protection of rights (KVKK Art.5/2-e)

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for the continuation of the session, remembering preferences, ensuring site performance and security, anonymous analytics, and, if applicable, marketing/retargeting purposes. You can change your cookie preferences at any time via the panel presented on first entry or through your browser settings.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for basic functions, cannot be disabled.
  • Performance/Analytics: Produce anonymous statistics to improve the site.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences (language, theme, etc.).
  • Marketing: Used for advertising/retargeting if consent is given.

You can block/delete cookies in your browser; however, some functions may be affected. To learn more about our cookie policy, click here.

9. Transfers to Third Parties and Data Processors

Your personal data may be transferred only for the purposes specified in this policy and to the extent necessary to the following recipient groups:

  • Hosting/CDN and infrastructure providers: e.g., hosting, content delivery network, firewall services.
  • Communication and form services: e.g., email delivery, contact form transmission services.
  • Analytics/Logging services: Production of performance, security, and usage statistics.
  • Competent authorities and institutions: Within the scope of legal obligations and court/administrative requests.

Note: We do not sell your data. Processing activities are carried out within the scope of contracts and technical/administrative measures with our suppliers in the status of “data processors.”

  • Hosting/CDN: Cloudflare
  • Form submission: Formspree
  • Font/asset delivery: Google Fonts (only on request may technical data such as IP and user-agent be processed)

10. International Data Transfers

Depending on server/supplier locations, your personal data may be transferred to countries outside Türkiye. In such cases, the appropriate safeguard mechanism among adequacy decisions, binding corporate rules, standard contractual clauses (SCCs), or safeguards determined by the Authority under KVKK is applied; your explicit consent is obtained when necessary.

11. Retention Periods

Data are retained for periods limited to processing purposes and as stipulated by legislation; when the period expires, they are securely deleted, destroyed, or anonymized.

12. Security Measures

  • Appropriate encryption and transport security (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access control, authorization, and authentication processes
  • Least privilege principle and logging/monitoring
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability testing
  • Contractual and technical security measures with data processors

In the event of any suspected personal data breach, an impact assessment is conducted; where necessary, notifications are made to the relevant individuals and competent authorities.

13. Artificial Intelligence, Profiling, and Automated Decisions

While developing our AI systems, we use anonymized/pseudonymized data wherever possible. Solely automated decisions that would produce legal or similarly significant effects on you are not made. Marketing activities based on profiling are conducted only with your consent, and you can opt out at any time.

14. Your Rights (KVKK Art.11 and, if Applicable, GDPR)

  • Learn whether your data are processed and request information
  • Learn whether they are processed in line with the purpose and the third parties to whom they are transferred domestically/abroad
  • Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Request deletion/destroying under conditions stipulated by legislation
  • Request restriction of processing or object
  • Object to automated processing and profiling
  • Withdraw explicit consent at any time
  • Data portability (for users under the GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Authority/relevant supervisory authority

15. Application Method (Data Subject Request)

You can send your requests regarding your rights to info@briqmind.com. You must provide the information required for identity verification and clearly state your request. As a rule, applications are concluded within no later than 30 days.

16. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware of such a situation, the relevant data will be deleted within a reasonable time.

This policy may be revised from time to time in line with technological developments, changes in our business processes, or legislative updates. The current version is always published on this page and the “Last Updated” date is indicated at the top section.
