Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 18 October 2025

1. Introduction and Purpose of the Policy

As BRIQ Mind ("BRIQ", "we"), we attach great importance to the privacy and data security of users who visit our website (www.briqmind.com). This Cookie Policy has been prepared to explain transparently which cookies we use on our site, why we use these cookies, and how you can control these technologies. By using our site, you accept the use of cookies as described in this policy.

This policy is an integral part of our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

2. What is a Cookie?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. These files remember your preferences related to your visit (for example language selection, session information) and help provide a more personalized experience on your next visit. Cookies serve various functions such as enabling a site to operate more efficiently and providing site owners with analytics information.

3. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies on our website for the following primary purposes:

  • Ensure the Basic Functionality of the Site: We use strictly necessary cookies for core features such as secure navigation and form submission.
  • Improve Performance and User Experience: We use analytics cookies to analyze how our site is used (for example which pages are visited most) to improve performance and provide a smoother experience.
  • Personalize and Market Our Services: We use marketing cookies to provide content and ads more relevant to your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Both first party cookies (placed directly by us) and third party cookies (placed by our partners) are used on our site. We classify these cookies by function as follows:

4.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are required for our website to operate securely and for core functions to be performed. Without these cookies the site will not function properly. They perform critical tasks such as managing the user session, taking security measures, and network management. Your consent is not requested for these cookies and they cannot be disabled.

4.2. Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to collect information anonymously such as how many people visit our site, how long visitors stay on which pages, and which links they click. We use this data to measure our site's performance, identify weak points, and continuously improve the overall user experience. Not accepting these cookies will limit our efforts to optimize our site's performance.

4.3. Marketing and Targeting Cookies

These cookies may be placed on our site by advertising partners. They are used by these companies to create a profile of your interests and to show you relevant advertisements on other sites. They do not directly store personal information but are based on the unique identity of your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will encounter less targeted advertising.

6. How Can You Manage Your Cookie Preferences?

We offer several options for managing your cookie preferences.

6.1. Through Our Website

You can change your preferences at any time via the cookie notice panel that appears when you first visit our site or by clicking the "Manage Cookies" link in the footer of our website. In this panel you can enable or disable cookie categories other than those that are strictly necessary.

6.2. Through Browser Settings

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies automatically. However you can change your browser settings to reject cookies, accept cookies only from specific sites, or have your browser warn you when a cookie is being stored. You can find instructions on how to change your browser settings at the links below:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

Please note that blocking all cookies through your browser may cause some features on our site not to function properly.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time due to changes in technology or legal regulations. We will announce significant changes to the policy on our site and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page. Your continued use of our site after changes indicates that you accept the updated policy.
