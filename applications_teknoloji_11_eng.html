Smart Systems (Tool)

Smart Systems (Tool)

Give artificial intelligence not only knowledge, but also the ability to act.

Beyond Conversation: Task Completing AI

There is a revolutionary difference between asking an AI “How many units of product X are left in stock?” and saying “If there are fewer than 10 units of product X in stock, automatically place an order for 50 units with the supplier.” Traditional AI can answer the first question, but it cannot execute the second. Smart Systems remove this barrier by giving AI the ability to use your company’s existing software, databases, and APIs as tools. This makes AI not only a source of information, but an active task executor.

The BRIQ Mind Solution: Digital Employees Equipped with Capabilities

We prepare a dedicated digital toolbox for your AI models. Each tool performs a specific function such as pulling data from a database, registering a new customer in the CRM, adding a meeting to the calendar, or sending an email. When a user makes a request, our smart system not only understands the request but also autonomously decides which tools to use and in what order to complete it, then executes.

Our Core Capabilities

  • Custom Tool Development: We design tools tailored to you that can communicate with your existing systems and APIs and integrate them into AI.
  • Intelligent Tool Selection and Orchestration: We enable AI to use multiple tools in a smart sequence to complete a task.
  • Real-time Data Access and Processing: We ensure that AI works not only with static information but with your live company data to perform instant and accurate actions.
  • Complex Multi-step Task Execution: Systems that can execute complex commands such as “List the top 5 best-selling products, check their stock levels, and send a report email to the purchasing department for those at critical levels.”

The Strategic Advantages for Your Business

A capable AI is the peak of efficiency and automation in your processes.

  • Truly Smart Assistants: Let your employees handle tasks in seconds with commands like “Find the last order of customer X and send me its invoice as a PDF.”
  • Interactive and Solution Oriented Customer Service: Build autonomous systems that work 24/7 where your customers can check order status, start a return process, or book an appointment.
  • End to End Process Automation: Fully automate complex workflows that require human intervention and span multiple departments and software such as the new employee onboarding process.
  • Data Driven Instant Actions: Ensure systems not only generate reports but also take immediate action based on the data in those reports such as stopping a low performing ad.
